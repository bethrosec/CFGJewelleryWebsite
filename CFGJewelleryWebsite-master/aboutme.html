About Me

Lucy specialises in Victorian and Georgian Antique treasures. She began buying Jewellery at sixteen years old and her love and passion for all things old has continued since that very first introduction.

She can be found every Saturday at Portobello road delighting and charming visitors with her knowledge and passion.
